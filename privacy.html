Introduction

PRIYA PATEL ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Settings mobile application (the "App") for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

The App is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account to use the App. We aim to minimize the data we collect. The categories of information that may be collected include:

  • Device Information: The App may access general device information (such as device model, operating system version, and screen resolution) solely to display this information to you and to ensure the App functions correctly on your device.
  • Usage and Diagnostics: We may collect limited, anonymized analytics and diagnostic data to help us understand how the App is used, identify issues, and improve performance. This data does not personally identify you.
  • Purchase Information: If you subscribe to premium features, purchase-related data (such as transaction receipts) may be processed for the purpose of validating your subscription. Payments are handled entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's privacy practices.

How Information Is Used

Any information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App's features and functionality.
  • To validate subscriptions and in-app purchases.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app stability.
  • To understand general usage patterns through anonymized analytics.

We do not directly sell your personal data to third parties.

Subscriptions and Purchases

The App may offer auto-renewable subscriptions and other in-app purchases. All transactions are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment details (such as credit card numbers). Subscription validation may involve communicating with Apple's servers to confirm the status of your purchase.

Third-Party Services

The App may use the following categories of third-party services:

  • Apple: The App is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect data in accordance with its own privacy policy, including data related to app downloads, purchases, and diagnostics.
  • Analytics Providers: We may use third-party analytics tools to collect anonymized usage data. These providers process data on our behalf and are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Subscription Validation Services: We may use third-party services to verify the status of subscriptions and purchases made through the App Store.

Data Retention

We retain collected data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Anonymized analytics data may be retained in aggregate form for a longer period to support ongoing improvements to the App. You may request the deletion of any personal data associated with you by contacting us.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information associated with the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

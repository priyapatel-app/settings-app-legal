About the App

Settings is a device companion app for iOS. It helps you view detailed device information, access helpful actions, review iOS-related information, and use premium utility features — all in one clean, easy-to-use interface.

Common Questions

What can I do with Settings?
View your device details (model, storage, battery, software version, and more), access useful shortcuts and actions, and unlock premium features with an optional subscription.
Do I need to create an account?
No. Settings does not require you to create an account or sign in. You can start using the app immediately after downloading it.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Settings is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not sell your personal data. For full details, please review our Privacy Policy.
What devices are supported?
Settings is available for iPhone and iPad running a supported version of iOS or iPadOS. Check the App Store listing for specific compatibility requirements.

How to Restore Purchases

If you have previously purchased a subscription or premium feature and need to restore it (for example, after reinstalling the app or switching to a new device):

  1. Open the Settings app.
  2. Navigate to the subscription or premium section.
  3. Tap "Restore Purchases".
  4. Sign in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase if prompted.

Your purchases should be restored automatically. If you experience any issues, ensure you are signed in with the correct Apple ID and have an active internet connection.

How to Manage Subscriptions

All subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To view, change, or cancel your subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad (the system Settings app).
  2. Tap your name at the top of the screen.
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Select the Settings app subscription.
  5. From here you can change your plan or tap Cancel Subscription.

Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.

Troubleshooting

The app is not loading or is crashing.
Try closing the app completely and reopening it. If the issue persists, restart your device. Make sure you are running the latest version of the app and that your iOS is up to date.
My subscription is not recognized.
Try restoring your purchases using the steps above. Ensure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase. If the problem continues, check your subscription status in your Apple ID settings.
Device information appears incorrect or incomplete.
The app relies on system-provided data to display device information. Some details may vary depending on your device model and iOS version. If you notice a significant discrepancy, please contact us.
I was charged but didn't receive premium features.
Please try restoring purchases first. If the issue is not resolved, contact Apple Support for billing inquiries, or reach out to us directly for further assistance.
